Souls D20 is a full-system table top roll playing experience inspired by the Elden Ring and Soulsborne games.

With strategic combat and highly customizable character builds, Souls D20 can be played in person or online using the Handbook for a complete ruleset as well as resources.

The full book includes over 190 pages of resources, such as spells lists, equipment and weapons lists, skill and ability trees, quick guides for leveling and building a character!

Customized Builds

21 unique trees to mix and match abilities and synergies from!

As you gain experience through adventuring your character will gain points to invest into multiple ability trees at once. Mix and match abilities from different trees to create a unique character and build.

Most abilities are usable with most weapon types! For example, a character that has invested into the Daggers tree to do more damage from behind, will be able to take advantage of that ability even when they are wielding a weapon other than a dagger.

Choose from a list of dozens of feats of destiny, which unlike weapon feats, can be invested into at any level by any character. Feats of destiny include the ability to become truly undead and take permanent injuries instead of dying, being able to speak new languages and communicate with any monster, and have a magical familiar which is bound and controlled by you.

Combine Hammers and Miracles to make a character that wields cold blue lightning to freeze their enemies and smash them into the ground!

Go down the Fist and Reaper tree to create a monk like character which deals extra bleed status effect build up in exchange for draining their own blood. Or a martial artist that defends their teammates while draining their enemies.

Try out a tanky gunslinger that defends with a shield while taking precise shots with a gun.

Wide Range Of Spells

Explore multiple categories of magic and spellcasting to build a caster or spellblade of your own! Sorceries, Miracles, Pyromancy, Hexes, and even Spirit Summoning. Each with multiple sub categories and ability trees to progress down.

Conjure the Hexes of death to explode corpses and create toxic fogs over the battlefield.

Use force miracles to push enemies and allies around into more advantageous positions.

Cover yourself in a veil of transparency and summon magic daggers to stab creatures in the back with assassin sorcery spells.

Capture the spirits of monsters you fight in battle, summoning them back to perform a wide variety of spirit abilities.

Master Weapon Skills

Don't let spellcasters have all the fun! Learn and take advantage of an array of weapon skills which you can mix and match to use with a wide variety of weapons.

Augment your attacks with area of effect to more easily hit multiple targets. Inspire and buff your allies with weapon skills.

Feel the power and utility of creating blades of air when you attack, pulling in enemies with the power of gravity, vaulting through the air to close gaps, and summoning a phantom of yourself to attack an unreachable target. These are just a few of the skills to discover!
