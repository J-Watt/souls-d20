The Frozen Warriors Festival (one shot session)

The city of Olafis sits in an arid land dotted with tall mesas and spires of rock. As a gateway between the desert empire of Erume and the rest of the continent of Archea, Olafis has been an important center of trade and travel. To celebrate this, a warriors festival is thrown where outsiders may test their strength and fighting styles against the best of the city. But now, Olafis stands on the brink of ruin. The frost dragon Acire along with a few thousand soldiers from the north swept through and took the city for themselves. Twisted to be a symbol of oppression, the warriors festival is now a way to get rid of prisoners and those who stand against the invaders. You and your group find yourselves trapped in this torturous colosseum, seeking freedom or even revenge.

Tales of the Tomb Prospectors (short campaign)

East of the kingdom of Yhelcain stands the beautiful city of Alvina. Trapped in perpetual autumn and built into the massive golden leaved trees. Alvina is home to a guild of adventurers tasked with delving deep into the southern ruins of Lethrell, which fell generations ago to unspeakable horrors. The ruins of Lethrell are home to powerful artifacts and magic items, some of which are to powerful to pass up or too dangerous to leave in the hands of others. Take your group of Tomb Prospector initiates and delve into the catacombs and dungeons of the ruins, and either come back rich with a haul of twisted treasures, or be driven mad at what you witness below.

The last Privateers of Erume (short campaign)

For the last decade or so, the great western ocean of Sarda Cen has been off limits to almost all travel. Entire fleets of ships go missing, with not even a single surviving soul to tell the tale of what happened. But amidst the chaos of war with the north, one last ship has been secretly commissioned to embark from the port of the golden capitol of the empire of Erume. Crewed with mercenaries and sailors that are mad enough for the journey, and tasked with discovering any glimpse of hope for reopening the waters or turning the tide of war, the last ship must sail on cursed waters and search for answers. Being part of this ship your group must stick together and brave the anomalous and mind melting journey with only each other, and those aboard that remain sane enough to rely on.
